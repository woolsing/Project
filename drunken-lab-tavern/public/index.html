📜 How to Play

The Goal

Be the first player to land 3 KOs — knock out 3 opponents by pushing their Intox meter to 100.

Intox Meter

Every player starts at 0 intox. Attacks raise it. If it hits 100, you collapse — the attacker earns a KO, and you recover at 20 intox after 4 seconds.

Each Turn

  • Draw 1 card at the start of your turn.
  • Play 1 card (or Pass Turn).
  • Some cards charge an intox "cost" — that's the drink you take to play them.

The Cards

  • ATTACK Cheap Beer — Deal 10 intox to a player.
  • ATTACK Shots! — Drink 10, deal 25 to a player.
  • ATTACK Chug! — Drink 20, deal 40 (55 if you're already above 60 intox).
  • HEAL Water Break — Reduce your own intox by 20.
  • CONTROL Sabotage — Drink 15, skip a player's next turn.
  • AOE Buy a Round! — Drink 5, hit every other player for 12.

Reactions

During the game, tap any emoji at the bottom to throw it at the table — visible to everyone, whether it's your turn or not. Short cooldown to keep it civil.
